About Us

“Shree Kashi Exports” the is the best name for high-quality fabrics since 1998. As a distinguished Manufacturer and Supplier, we specialize in offering a diverse range of fabrics catering to various needs. From Rib Knit Trim Fabric to Sports Wear Trim Collar and Cuffs, Party Wear Jacket Rib Knit Trim to Sandwich Collar and Cuffs, we take pride in providing products that meet the highest quality and durability standards. With a wide distribution network spanning across India and internationally, we have successfully reached diverse markets, serving clients with varying needs and preferences. Whether it's for fashion, sports, or special occasions, our fabrics are crafted to inspire creativity and elevate the final product.

As a Sole Proprietorship entity, Shree Kashi Exports is a well-known company for manufacturing and supplying committed to delivering exceptional fabrics that exceed customer expectations, and that commitment remains at the core of everything we do.
